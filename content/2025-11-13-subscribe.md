Title: How to subscribe to the PDX-LKMU calendar feed
Date: 2025-11-13 23:30
Category: Status
Author: Ian Kluft

Whichever calendaring system you use, you can subscribe to the Portland Linux Kernel Meetup calendar
by adding any one of the following URLs as a calendar. You will then automatically get our events in your
calendar soon after they get posted.

  * [https://ikluft.github.io/pdx-lkmu/calendar.ics](https://ikluft.github.io/pdx-lkmu/calendar.ics) (direct from our site, this gets updated first before importing to Calagator)
  * [https://calagator.org/events/search.ics?tag=PDXLKMU](https://calagator.org/events/search.ics?tag=PDXLKMU)
  * [webcal://calagator.org/events/search.ics?tag=PDXLKMU](webcal://calagator.org/events/search.ics?tag=PDXLKM)
